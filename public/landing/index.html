About Me

My name is Satyaprada Radhika devi dasi. My journey in Carnatic music began at the age of 5, under the guidance of my mother, an accomplished classical singer. By age 7, I continued my musical education with a dedicated teacher who profoundly transformed my approach to music. Over the next decade, I honed my skills, mastering various vocal techniques, composing original pieces, and excelling in numerous competitions.

Due to my upbringing in a Krishna concious family, I was also fortunate to learn various Vaishnava Bhaajans written by Acaryas of the Gaudiya Sampradaya. This led me to learn classical instruments like mrdanga and harmonium. I have had the opportunity to sing for the Lord in over 200 occasions.

About 5 years ago, I began teaching these bhajans and kirtans to both children and adults. Now, I am eager to spread the love of music to a wider audience. Join us and start your journey today!
